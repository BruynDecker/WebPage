Tableau Data

This page was made for a school project to display tableau graphs and charts from a dataset.

How are profits faring in each of the 50 states?

This map shows the total profits in each state, with states doing better colored blue, and states doing worse colored orange. The state with the most profits is California. The state with the second-most is New York, and the others are not even close.


How has one of the highest-profit states fared for the last two years by profit, shown by month?

California is the state with highest profits. Here we see their sales for the past two years. Looks like their best month was November 2013. In general, profits seem be lower at the beginning of the year, begin to rise at the end of the summer, and then peak at the end of the year.

How are profits within that state doing for each product category?

Office Supplies by far pull in the most profits in California. They account for almost half of all profits. Technology is second and furniture is third.

For the most profitable category, forecast sales (pick a state that has enough data for a forecast) through November 2015

This is the forecast, including the data Tableau used to make its prediction. The trend of slower business in the first two quarters, with business picking up in the final two quarters, will continue. However, it looks like Tableau also picked up on a general uptrend, meaning that the coming year will see us getting our best numbers yet!
